About Origen

Our engineers assist you throughout our partnership. Their expertise and their involvement in technological developments ensures the responsiveness you deserve.






Founded in 2004, Origen has been providing individually tailored Fiber
Internet and complete VoIP solutions for medium to large scale
businesses all over Canada. We aim to enhance our services with our approach towards customer satisfaction, reasonable pricing, andvbr much more.








At Origen, we strive to provide a perfect business experience through our products and services. Our highly qualified staff is more than ready to assist you through your alliance with us. Their vast knowledge in technological developments is sure to provide you the accessibility and benefits you deserve. Origen is proud to hold a reputable name in the business internet and communication market throughout Canada. Upon collaborating with us, businesses can chose from our wide range of products and services and avail them for a constant and healthy growth of their enterprise. At Origen, our focus is completely centered on enterprise communications and providing high speed Fiber internet to them. We develop and deliver novel products, applications, and solutions that are specifically personalized according to the core needs of each valuable client. Our company is flexible in a way that it provides features as simple as call managers for small and medium businesses, rising up to the level of large companies requiring more extensive solutions such as integrated mobility, call centre solutions, and choosing from a wide range of terminals. Our target is to enable seamless communications between businesses.

After laying its roots in 2004 in Montreal, Canada, Origen has succeeded in becoming a leading company in the telecommunications sector. We benefit not only large and reputable businesses, but also provide services feasibility to small and medium scaled businesses, giving each one an equally advantageous experience. With an increasing tech-oriented world out there, staying connected is a sine qua non for all industries, irrespective of their type. This is where Origen steps in, packed with high quality and low maintenance telecom products, services and business IP Telephony, including the latest VoIP phones and IP Phones, ready to boost the growth of any company. Another great benefit businesses can avail is adaptability. According to the specific needs per client, Origen solutions suit their mobility, financial, and security needs. An unlimited local and long distance telephone services and systems and teleworker compatibility, no initial capital investment is needed to avail our services. Regarding our services concerning ISP (Internet Service Providers), we do not resell, but instead provide our own services. Our VoIP system is highly understanding of your basic needs to be able to work to its full potential.
